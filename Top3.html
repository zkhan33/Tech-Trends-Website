Top 3 Technology Trends of 2021


As each day is passing by, we are being introduced to new forms of technology. Whether it be cars driving and parking on its own or having apps suggesting material by tracking user activity. With the advancement of technology, I wanted to create a miniature website of the top trending forms of technology of 2021. Before entering 2022, a new year that will surpass the previous, with better and advanced technologies, let's have a look back at 2021s latest technologies!


Tesla's Parking Assist Feature

Tesla has added a new feature known as the Parking Assist. The Parking Assist will park your car for you, and yes, that includes parallel parking as well! All you need to do is pull up to the parking stall of your choice, wait until you see a blue logo with a 'P' in it, put your car in reverse, then just hit start and let the car do its magic! However, do stay alert for any possible mishaps! To learn more visit Parking Assist.

Virtual Reality Gaming

Virtual reality(VR) games are now being seen on mostly every social media platform. The games vary from Half-Life:Alyx to Batman and to many other virtual reality games. VR games is basically the chance for you to 'actually' be present in a game! You get to interact with differnt pieces of the games as if you were actually in it! You can do all sorts of motions. Such as running, moving arms, picking up items, etc. Getting very popular as more games are released.

Facial Recognition

Facial recognition is now becoming the next big thing in technological devices. Iphone has implemented this invention back in 2017, for the release of the Iphone X. Since then, it's been added to multile other sorts of devices! It's set up with having your face in the center of the camera and moved all around in 360 degrees to get every angle. It then just unlocks once it recognizes your face. It basically is a substiture for a password and if you happen to forget your password, and facial recognition is set up, you can then unlock your device without any issues!
